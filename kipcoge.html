WHO IS ELIUD KIPCHOGE ?

Eliud Kipchoge (born 5 November 1984) is a Kenyan long-distance runner who competes in the marathon and formerly the 5000 metres. He won the Olympic marathon in 2016 and is the current marathon world record holder with a time of 2:01:39 hours. Kipchoge's world record run at the 2018 Berlin Marathon broke the previous world record by 1 minute and 18 seconds.[1] This is the greatest improvement in a marathon world record time since 1967.
